## Teaching Philosophy

I believe in **bridging theory and practice** in engineering education. My approach combines rigorous mathematical foundations with hands-on implementation, helping students understand not just *how* things work, but *why* they work. Having worked at the intersection of classical control and modern machine learning, I strive to show students the connections between different areas of engineering.
