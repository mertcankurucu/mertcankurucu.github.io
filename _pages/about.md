I am a recent **Ph.D. graduate** from the Department of Control and Automation Engineering at Istanbul Technical University, where I also serve as a Research and Teaching Assistant. I completed my doctoral studies in January 2025 under the supervision of [Prof. Müjde Güzelkaya](https://web.itu.edu.tr/guzelkaya/) and [Prof. Ibrahim Eksin](https://web.itu.edu.tr/eksin/), focusing on **fractional-order derivative based adaptive optimization and control methods**.

My research interests lie at the intersection of **classical control theory** and **modern machine learning**, with a particular focus on:

- **Adaptive Robust Learning**: Developing fractional calculus-based robust loss functions for handling noise in large-scale deep learning datasets
- **Fractional Control Systems**: Theoretical analysis and real-world implementation of variable-order fractional controllers
- **Deep Reinforcement Learning**: Applications to structural engineering and seismic control
- **Graph Neural Networks**: Influential sample selection and multigraph population fusion for brain connectome analysis

I have published **9 peer-reviewed papers** in top-tier journals and conferences, including Knowledge-Based Systems, IEEE Transactions on Emerging Topics in Computational Intelligence, and International Journal of Systems Science. My work spans both theoretical contributions and practical applications, with open-source implementations available on [GitHub](https://github.com/mertcankurucu).

Currently, I serve as **Managing Editor** for Springer's *Advances in Computational Intelligence* journal and have collaborated with leading international research groups, including the BASIRA Lab at Imperial College London. I am proficient in MATLAB, Python, PyTorch, and have extensive experience with control systems design and deep learning frameworks.

I am actively seeking **postdoctoral research opportunities** to further develop my expertise in robust machine learning and its applications to complex dynamical systems. My goal is to bridge the gap between control theory and modern AI, contributing to more reliable and interpretable learning systems.

**Recent Achievement**: I was awarded the Publication Award as one of the 'Highest Performing Research Assistants' from Istanbul Technical University in 2023-2024.
